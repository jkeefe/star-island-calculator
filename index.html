Unofficial LOAS Cost Calculator

by John Keefe

The Life on a Star conferences are wonderful weeks of relaxation and learning. But calculating the total costs of going, and the amount due on the island, can be a challenge. I decided to turn that challenge into a coding project. The below is based on 2015 values from this page. It doesn't include partial-weeks or charges folks might rack up on the island. I found it super useful, but it's not official, could be out of date and/or contain errors. Please let me know at john (at) johnkeefe (dot) net if you find any!
