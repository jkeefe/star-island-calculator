Unofficial LOAS Cost Calculator

by John Keefe

The Life on a Star conferences are wonderful, but calculating the cost of going, the deposits and the amount you'll need to pay on the island can be tricky.

I decided to turn that challenge into a coding project. The below is based on 2016 conference fees, room rates, deposits and parking costs from this page. It doesn't include partial weeks or charges incurred on the island. I found it helpful for our family, but it's not official, could be out of date and/or just plain wrong Please let me know at john (at) johnkeefe (dot) net if you find any errors!
